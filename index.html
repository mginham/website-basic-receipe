Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Grilled Cheese Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 2 Sliced bread
  • 1 Sliced cheese
  • 1/2 tablespoon Salted butter

Grilled Cheese Preparation

Directions:

  1. Place the slice of cheese inbetween the slices of bread
  2. Spread butter on both of the outter sides of the bread
  3. Heat a pan over medium-low heat
  4. Add the uncooked sandwich to the pan and cook until bottom slice of bread is golden-brown
  5. Flip the sandwich and cook until golden-brown
